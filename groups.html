SWC Partners


Kids Alley

Kids Alley is an organization based in Pennsauken where volunteers can spend their time volunteering with kids from all over Camden County. After school, volunteers can go and help the kids with their homework and then play games and talk with them. This is an amazing way to get envolved in your community and some really special bonds are formed with kids (make sure to fill out the volunteer application of their website!); they work out of 4106 Terrace Avenue, Pennsauken, NJ.




Urban Promise

The Urban Promise mission is "to equip Camden's children and young adults with the skills necessary for academic achievement, life management, spiritual growth, and Christian leadership." They strive to achieve this goal through the use of after-school programs, summer camps, job trainign, etc. In the past Stand With Camden has been most involved with the street leader program and, relatedly, funding Noah through private school.


Cathedral Kitchen

Stand with Camden often hosts sandwich making nights at Haddonfied Memorial High School, and all the sandwiches made are donated to Cathedral Kitchen. In the past, some members have also been able to go in person and help out as well as bringing the donations

Camden County Pop-Up Library

Stand With Camden works closely with Tom Martin, the leader of CCPUL, to aid in the CCPUL mission: providing open and direct access to books in impoverished or underserved communities. We often volunteer with Tom at his Pop-Up Libraries outside of Cathedral Kitchen In Camden. Here we socialize with those passing by and help them find and choose books they are interested in.
