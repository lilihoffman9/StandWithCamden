Kids Alley

Kids Alley is an organization based in Pennsauken where volunteers can spend their time tutoring kids from all over Camden County.

After school, volunteers can help the kids with their homework, talk and play with them. This is an amazing way to get envolved in your community and some really special bonds are formed with kids!

You can volunteer any day Monday-Thursday from 3-5 in their building at 4106 Terrace Avenue, Pennsauken, NJ.

Make sure to fill out the volunteer application of their website and have an image of your vaccination card!

For more information and the application form, visit the partners page!
