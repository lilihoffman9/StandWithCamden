Pop-Up Libraries

The Pop-Up Library is a free library that is set up outside of Cathedral Kitchen with Tom Martin from Camden County Pop-Up Libraries.

Stand With Camden volunteers work at the Pop-Up Libraries with Tom and give out books to those visiting Cathedral Kitchen. This is another amazing community service experinece and is touching for both the volunteer and those who visit the library.

Dates for these events are shared via remind, but feel free to reach out to a volunteer with any questions and/or future Pop-Up Library dates.
