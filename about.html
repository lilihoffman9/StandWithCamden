About the Club


Mission Statement

Stand With Camden works toward improving education and life for citizens throughout Camden County by raising awareness for and volunteering with various revitalization projects and learning opportunities with students within Camden County.

Our Start

Stand With Camden's first project was sponsoring Noah, a student in Camden County, through high school through the organization Urban Promise. While SWC does still work with Urban Promise, the partner organizations, members, and volunteering opportunities have grown more than ever was thought possible.
