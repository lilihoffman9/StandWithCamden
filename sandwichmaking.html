Sandwich Making Nights

At Sandwich Making Nights, we make sandwiches for 1-2 hours for Cathedral Kitchen in Camden. This started with SWC and they are usually our largest events. We aim to get as many people in the SWC (and LEO) club to attend as possible!

This is a very fun and impactful event that is so easy to participate in. Reach out to one of the officers if you would like more details and/or the date of the next sandwich making night.

We strongly encourage those who attend to bring 1+ loafs of bread as that is usually what we run out of first!
